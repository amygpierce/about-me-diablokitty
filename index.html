Biography

I am a recovering accountant (who sometimes relapses during tax season), who decided that a second act as a developer would be a great way to lose whatever sanity I have left. I am the daughter of a GM Systems Analyst, and the granddaughter of one of the original 'punchcard girls' at the University of Chicago's first computer lab, so you could say that code is in my genes.
